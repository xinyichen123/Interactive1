The environmental impact of 2°C riset

At 2°Cs warming, some places will see an increase in heavy rainfall events compared to at 1.5°C warming. This includes Eastern North America, which will see higher flooding risks. Other affected areas include the Northern Hemisphere high latitudes (Alaska/Western Canada, Eastern Canada/Greenland/Iceland, Northern Europe, Northern Asia) and Southeast Asia. It up to 4°C (7.2 degrees Fahrenheit) warmer at 2°Cwarming. At 2°C warming, the deadly heatwaves India and Pakistan saw in 2015 may occur annually.
